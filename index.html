Aim

The Standard Bank Buy-a-Brick initiative is a flagship corporate social investment campaign geared towards addressing the country’s housing needs for disadvantaged communities through the Shack Dwellers Federation of Namibia as well as supporting the Harambee Prosperity Plan endorsed by our President Dr. Hage Geingob.
